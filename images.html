For this image I created a collage of different images because I want to use it as a banner on the webpage. I created the collage by creating guides on the artboard. After that, I copied the chosen image and with the paste special/paste into I pasted the image in the selected area. The area was selected using the marquee tool. After that I optimised the image for the web. I then decided to go down in size and saved as a JPG lower resolution with a size of 81.2 KB. I did manage to keep the image resolution like the original one with very little loss.
